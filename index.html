Our Team

Doron Rotem - CEO

With a B.Sc in Physics and an M.Sc in Electronics Engineering, Doron has accumulated extensive experience in executive positions at Israel Aerospace Industries (IAI) for more than 30 years.

His expertise includes:

  • Leading business management with major companies worldwide
  • Managing people and finances in high-complexity organizations
  • Leading procurement processes in large-scale operations

Public Positions:

  • Chairman of the Board of the Israeli Purchasing & Logistic Managers Association
  • Member of the International organization IPLMA

Prior Experience:

  • Marketing director in the U.S. for advanced space and defense products
  • Board director in startup companies in Israel and the U.S.
  • CEO of Ofek International Aerospace, leading business in aviation and defense internationally

Currently, Doron leads Ofek's focus on inspiring and supporting startup companies, providing both financial and technical support for new technical challenges.
